Meet Bhupendra Negi

Hello, I'm Bhupendra Negi, founder of Negi Dream Studio. Over the past decade since 2013, I've passionately transformed moments into timeless memories. Starting my photography journey at 40, I find inspiration in the cultural richness of Masi in Almora, Uttarakhand.

Photography is not just a profession; it's my lifelong passion. From serene Uttarakhand landscapes to vibrant celebrations in Haldwani and Almora, I've joyfully captured weddings, engagements, birthdays, and more. At 50, I infuse every photograph with the same enthusiasm.

Explore my unique style on the portfolio page and let's create magic together on your special day. I'm not just a photographer; I'm a storyteller dedicated to weaving narratives of your unique moments.
